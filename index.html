I am a Linguistics PhD candidate at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where I work with Dr. Silvina Montrul. I am interested in heritage language acquisition of Turkish in the United States.

I graduated with a BA (Hons.) in Foreign Language Education from Boğaziçi University, İstanbul, Turkey in 2015. In 2018, I received my first Masters in Foreign Language Education from Boğaziçi University, where I was advised by Dr. Ayşe Gürel. I did my second Masters thesis under the supervision of Dr. Silvina Montrul at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. For more details, check my CV or contact me on my email.
